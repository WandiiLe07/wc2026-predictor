Model Notes & Methodology

Algorithm: Gradient Boosting Classifier (GBM) for outcome prediction; Random Forest Regressors for scoreline prediction.

Training data: 16,052 competitive international matches from 1990–2026 including all FIFA World Cup finals, qualifiers, UEFA, CAF, AFC, CONMEBOL, and CONCACAF competitive fixtures.

Features: Rolling goals scored/conceded, win rate, draw rate, recency-weighted form points, experience index, home/away differentials, neutral ground flag.

Fair odds: Calculated as 1 / model probability. Real bookmaker odds will include a margin (~5–10%) — so if a bookie offers odds above the fair price, that's a value bet.
